Pasta Pesto

Description

Pesto pasta is easy to make and full of flavor. It tastes good hot or cold!

Ingridients

  • Pasta: Start with your favorite pasta shape.
  • Onion and oil: Cook the onion in olive oil until itâ€™s translucent.
  • Pesto: Use store-bought or homemade pesto sauce.
  • Seasonings: Salt and pepper.
  • Cheese: Grate your own Parmesan cheese.

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Cook pasta in salted water.
  3. Cook onion in oil.
  4. Stir in pesto, salt, and pepper.
  5. Combine pesto mixture with pasta and toss with cheese.
